Margin, Border & Padding

Whats the differnce?

In this blog we will be breaking down and going over the concepts known as margin, border & padding inside CSS. The before mentioned names are three of the four elements that make up what's known as a box model.

Here's a quick overview of each section

  • Content - At the core of our box we have the content that is being displayed
  • Padding - Provides a transparent space around your content
  • Border - defines the edge of your padding & margin
  • Margin - defines a transparent space around your border

Put another way the margin controls the space around your element, for example how close you want other elements to be to your border while the padding is defining how much space you want between your content and your border.
