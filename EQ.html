What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence (EQ) is the capacity to be aware, control and express ones emotions. Additionally is the ability to understand, interpret, and respond to the emotions of others as well.

How is it different to IQ?

Your (EQ) as defined above is in relation to your awareness of your own and other emotions while IQ is generally related to someone's intellectual & academic ability.

Why is it important?

EQ is important and has been argued to be more important & impactful on your life than IQ. Developing one's EQ skills can have wide reaching benefits across all aspects of your life, from improving how you work in teams/relationships, being better adapted to change, dealing with tough conversations and has been attributed to a key skill in positions of leadership.
